Tom Ash is a British singer-songwriter and accomplished acoustic fingerstyle guitarist based in Germany. His music is rooted in honest storytelling, introspective reflections and powerful songwriting. On stage, Tom brings these stories to life with engaging, entertaining performances.

Drawing inspiration from many musical influences, Tom has developed a distinctive and authentic sound. Through diverse playing techniques, alternative guitar tunings, percussive elements, and inventive use of effects, he creates a rich, layered sound that exceeds what one might expect from a solo acoustic guitar player.

Through a mix of original compositions and carefully reimagined covers, Tom creates shows that draw in and engage audiences of all kinds. Follow Tom's music on Bandcamp and YouTube to stay up to date with his latest releases and performances:
