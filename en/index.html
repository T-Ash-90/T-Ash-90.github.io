Tom Ash is a singer-songwriter from Nottingham, England. An accomplished acoustic fingerstyle guitarist, his songs tell stories that range from quiet, intimate reflections to powerful, captivating narratives.

Influenced by a wide spectrum of music, Tom has developed a distinctive and authentic sound. Through diverse playing techniques, alternative guitar tunings, percussive elements, and inventive use of effects, he creates a rich, layered sound that exceeds what one might expect from a solo acoustic guitar player. His music thrives on subtle dynamics, intricate nuances, and emotional depth.

Blending original compositions with uniquely reimagined covers, Tom creates performances that are emotionally powerful, and memorably immersive and entertaining. Every show is a journey.
